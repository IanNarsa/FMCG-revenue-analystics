## 📈 Executive Dashboard

In this project, I acted as a Lead BI Analyst to solve three critical business challenges for an Indonesian FMCG distributor: **margin erosion**, **portfolio bloat (SKU proliferation)**, and **channel partner inefficiencies**.

### **Key Metrics & Impact Summary**
* **Total Revenue Analyzed:** `Rp 2,583,524,000`
* **Total Gross Profit:** `Rp 484,489,000`
* **Overall Portfolio Margin:** `18.75%`
* **Pareto Disruption:** **60% of SKUs** (15 items) generate **80% of revenue**. The remaining 40% (10 items) are identified as "Dead Weight" dragging down margin performance.
* **Forecasted Q1 Recovery:** Modeled an upward recovery trend targeting `Rp 279M` (Jan), `Rp 289M` (Feb), and `Rp 299M` (Mar) in monthly sales.


## 🔍 Core Insights & Strategic Impact

### 1. **SKU Rationalization (Pareto & Growth-Share Analysis)**
* **The Problem:** The division was suffering from "long-tail" distribution inefficiencies. 40% of the product lineup contributed negligible revenue and low margins.
* **The Insight:** Identified **10 products** (mostly small-format dairy under 250ml/g like Cimory mini yoghurts and milks) as **"Dead Weight"**.
* **Strategic Action:** Recommended a delisting and shelf-space rationalization program to reallocate trade-marketing budget onto the **"Stars"** (such as *Anchor Cheddar Block 500g* yielding **11.27%** of entire profits, and *So Good Chicken Sausage 375g* yielding **11.05%** of entire profits).

### 2. **Sales Channel Mix Optimization**
* **The Problem:** Distributors dominated volume (55% revenue, Rp 1.43B), but their margins were compressed due to heavy discount structures.
* **The Insight:** **General Trade** channels yielded the highest average gross margin of **17.77%**, compared to Distributors (17.30%) and Modern Trade (17.50%).
* **Strategic Action:** Implement incentive programs to shift regional sales mixes towards General Trade in high-margin districts like **Yogyakarta (18.29% average margin)** and **West Java (17.95%)**.

### 3. **Direct-to-Institution Pipeline**
* **The Problem:** Institutional clients represented high-value, high-margin transactions (**18.19% margin**), but were entirely serviced through distributors.
* **Strategic Action:** Transition high-volume institutional accounts to a direct corporate sales model, capturing an estimated **200–300 bps** margin expansion.
